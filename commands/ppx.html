‚öôÔ∏è Working Principle

The command examines every possible pair of selected lines to find their intersection locations. For each intersecting pair, it calculates precise intersection coordinates and creates a point at that location.

Example: With 10 selected lines, the command examines 45 unique line pairs (10√ó9√∑2) and creates points at all intersection locations.
